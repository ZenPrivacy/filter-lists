! Description: Block feeds and other doomscroll-inducing elements on X (x.com). Compatible with AdGuard.

! /home feed
[$path=/home]x.com#?#div[data-testid="primaryColumn"] > div:last-child > div:nth-child(5):style(visibility: hidden !important)
! /explore feed
[$path=/explore]x.com#?#div[data-testid="primaryColumn"] > div > div:not(:has(form)):style(visibility: hidden !important)

! Sidebar sections
! "Today's News"
x.com##div[data-testid="news_sidebar"]:upward(1)
! "What's happening"
x.com##a[href="/explore/tabs/for-you"]:upward(6)
! "Who to follow"
x.com##div[data-testid="sidebarColumn"] a[href^="/i/connect_people"]:upward(3)
