! Description: Block feeds and other doomscroll-inducing elements on Instagram (instagram.com). Compatible with Zen and uBlock Origin.

# Feeds on /, /explore (not /explore/search), and /reels
instagram.com#?#:matches-path(/^\/$/) article:style(visibility: hidden !important)
instagram.com#?#:matches-path(/^\/explore\/$/) main > :not(nav)
instagram.com#?#:matches-path(/explore/people) main
instagram.com#?#:matches-path(/reels) main:style(visibility: hidden !important)

# /reels in navbar
instagram.com##a[href="/reels/"]
