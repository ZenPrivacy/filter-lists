! Description: Block feeds and other doomscroll-inducing elements on Instagram (instagram.com). Compatible with AdGuard.

# Feeds on /, /explore (not /explore/search), and /reels
[$path=/^\/$/]instagram.com#?#article:style(visibility: hidden !important)
[$path=/^\/explore\/$/]instagram.com##main > :not(nav)
[$path=/explore/people]instagram.com##main
[$path=/reels]instagram.com#?#main:style(visibility: hidden !important)

# /reels in navbar
instagram.com##a[href="/reels/"]
